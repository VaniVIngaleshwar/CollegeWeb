PCMB Career Options

PCMB is a combination of science subjects comprises of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology, which are taken in the first year of the Pre-University course. Students after successful completion of the PCMB Course in the second year PUC can choose either Engineering, Medical, or professional degree courses related to PCMB subjects. There are various competitive exam options after completion of the PCMB course, which include JEE (Engineering), NEET (Medical/Dental), BITSAT, COMED-K, NDA, and other state levels as well as national level entrance examinations.

Eligibility

  • Admission to PCMB Course in Bangalore is strictly based on the merits as per the norms of the Pre-University Board, the Government of Karnataka.
  • Students who have passed 10th/SSLC/or any other equivalent examination recognized by the PU Board Department are eligible for the admission.
  • PCMB course duration is two years

  • BDS ( Bachelor of Dental Surgery)
  • BAMS ( Bachelor of Ayurveda Medicine and Surgery)
  • Pharmacology
  • Bio- Medical engineering
